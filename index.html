Theoretical Foundation (EN)

The CAM proposes that the universe emerges from a chaotic substrate through local convergence. It is a system of recursive stabilization, not based on fixed laws but on shifting informational equilibriums.

Mathematical Model

CAM defines fields and states as informational approximations that evolve through convergence pressure. Time, gravity, and entropy are interpreted as functions of alignment delay and local informational structure.
